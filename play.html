Entangled

One move. Two dimensions. Infinite possibilities.

Basic Rules

On your turn, place 2 stones - one on each board - on matching symbols. Each symbol on one board has a matching symbol on the other board.

Your goal is to create the largest connected groups on both boards. Your final score is the sum of your largest group on each board.

Balanced Setup (Optional)

After the first move, the second player can choose to "swap" - taking over the first player's stone as their own. This balances first-move advantage.

Superposition (Optional)

Special stones that "collapse" when all adjacent (non-diagonal) spaces are filled. The stone takes the color of the majority of its neighbours. If tied, it takes the color of the last adjacent stone played.
