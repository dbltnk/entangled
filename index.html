Entangled

A 2-player abstract game inspired by quantum entanglement.

Setup

Two 5√ó5 boards are played in parallel. Each space has a matching symbol on both boards.

Starting

Black stone starts at center of Board 1, white stone at center of Board 2.

Playing

On your turn, place a stone on any empty space. A matching stone must be placed on the corresponding space on the other board.

Winning

Game ends after all stones are placed. Count your largest orthogonally connected group on each board. Add these numbers for your final score.
